PAINEWEBBER GROUP <PWJ> TO REDEEM DEBENTURES

Painewebber Group Inc said it will redeem all its outstanding 8-1/4 pct convertible subordinated debentures due 2008.     It said it will redeem all the debentures for 1,060.50 dlrs plus accrued interest to the redemption date of March 30. The notes can be converted into common stock at a price of 42.35 dlrs per share.
