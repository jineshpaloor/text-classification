UTILICORP <UCU> COMPLETES ACQUISITION

UtiliCorp United Inc said it completed the acquisition of West Virginia Power from Dominion Resources for about 21 mln dlrs.     The sale was approved by the West Virginia Public Service Commission in January and became effective March one. West Virginia's management will continue to be responsible for operating the utility, it said.  Reuter &#3;
