CARGILL U.K. STRIKE TALKS POSTPONED

Talks due today between management and unions to try to end the strike at Cargill U.K. Ltd's Seaforth oilseed crushing plant have been rescheduled for Thursday, a company spokesman said.     Oilseed processing at the plant has been halted since December 19 when mill workers walked out in protest at new contract manning levels.  Reuter &#3;
