ARCHIVE CORP <ACHV> SIGNS DISTRIBUTOR AGREEMENT

Archive Corp said it signed an agreement under which <Microamerica>, a value-added reseller of personal computers, peripherals and supplies, will distribute its Archive XL tape drive.    Archive said Microamerica will carry both models of the XL drive, a 40-megabyte, 5.25-inch drive based on the QIC-40 standard that is compatible with the IBM PC/XT and PC/AT PCs.  Reuter &#3;
