Liebert Corp said its shareholders approved the merger of a wholly-owned subsidiary of Emerson Electric Co <EMR>.     Under the terms of the merger, each Liebert shareholder will receive .3322 shares of Emerson stock for each Liebert share.  Reuter &#3;
