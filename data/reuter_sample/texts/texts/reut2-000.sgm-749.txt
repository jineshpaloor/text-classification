VIDEO SHOPPING MALL ADDS FLORIDA STATION

<Video Shopping Mall Inc> said it signed WJTC-TV, Pensacola, Fla, to carry its self-improvement and specialized business opportunity programs.     Video Shopping said the station, carried over UHF channel 44 in the Pensacola area, reaches about 120,000 households.  Reuter &#3;
