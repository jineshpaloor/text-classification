LODGISTIX <LDGX> TO BUYBACK 100,000 SHARES

Lodgistix Inc said its board authorized a program for the company to purchase up to 100,000 of its shares from time to time.     The company said the shares will be used for general purposes, including incentive programs.  Reuter &#3;
